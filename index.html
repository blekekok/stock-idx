WHAT IS IHSG AND LQ45?

IDX

The IDX (Indonesia Stock Exchange) is a vibrant marketplace where investors trade various securities, contributing to the growth and development of Indonesia's economy.

IHSG

IHSG (Indonesia Composite Index) is a market index in Indonesia that reflects the performance of companies, providing insights into the country's economic landscape.

LQ45

LQ45 is an index consisting of 45 liquid and actively traded stocks listed on the IDX, representing portion of the market and serving as a key indicator for investors in Indonesia.

BLUECHIP

Blue-chip stocks refer to well-established companies with a history of stable earnings and a strong market presence, often considered safe investments.

IHSG Strengthtening

The Composite Stock Price Index (JCI) strengthened 4.09% throughout 2022 and closed at 6,850.62 on Friday (30/12). This year's JCI gain is lower than 10.07% in 2021. JCI ended 2022 with a total market capitalization value of IDR 9,499 trillion, based on IDX data. The IDX's market capitalization value increased 15.06% this year even though the JCI only rose 4.09%. This much higher increase in market capitalization was mainly due to the addition of issuers through IPOs and the issuance of new shares, both through rights issues and private placements of old issuers. This year there are 59 new issuers that are holding an initial public offering (IPO). The total value of this year's IPO reached Rp 33.06 trillion.

IHSG Losses

The IDX Technology Sector weighed on the stock with a 42.61% decline since the start of the year. Meanwhile, the shares of PT GoTo Gojek Tokopedia Tbk (GOTO) are the biggest laggard or ballast for the JCI throughout 2022. PT Bank Jago Tbk (ARTO) shares were dropped from the list of 10 stocks with the largest capitalization this year. This digital bank's share price fell 76.8% and caused its market capitalization to plunge to only IDR 51 trillion. A similar fate befell the shares of PT Elang Mahkota Teknologi Tbk (EMTK). The issuer holding's share price fell 54.8%. EMTK's market capitalization was cut to Rp 63 trillion.
